Remembering Chadwick Boseman
November 29, 1976 - August 28, 2020

  • 1976 - Born in Anderson, South Carolina
  • 1995 - Graduated T.L. Hanna High School
  • 1998 - Completed the summer porgram of the British American Drama Academy at Balliol College Oxford
  • 2000 - Graduated Howard University with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in directing
  • 2000 - Boseman was named Drama League Directing Fellow
  • 2002 - Boseman performed in multiple productions and won an Audelco award
  • 2003 - Boseman's first television role was in an episode of First Watch. Where he was later fired for speaking out against racist stereotypes. He went on to play roles in Law & Order, Cold Case, CSI: NY, and ER
  • 2006 - Nominated for the Jeff Award for Best New Work on his play Deep Azure
  • 2008 - His short film Blood Over a Broken Pawn was honored at the Hollywood Black Film Festival
  • 2010 - Landed a regular role in the series Persons Unknown
  • 2013 - In film 42 Boseman had the lead role in portraying Jackie Robinson
  • 2014 - Boseman trained for two months eight hours a day so that he could portray James Brown in Get On Up
  • 2016 - Boseman began portraying T'Challa in Captain America: Civil War. He learned Xhosa and spoke it on and off the set
  • 2018 - Stared as the Black Panther in the movie Black Panther and it was one of the highest grosing films
  • 2019 Boseman secretly married singer Taylor Simone Ledward
  • 2016- Boseman was diagnosed with stage 111 colon cancer. It progressed to stage IV by 2020
  • Boseman handled his success and illness with grace. He had a profound impact worldwide.
  • Philanthropy - Boseman supported various charities

"We know what it's like to be told there isn't a screen for you to be featured on, a stage for you to be featured on. We know what it's like to be beneath and not above. And that is what we went to work with every day. We knew that we could create a world that exemplified a world we wanted to see. We knew that we had something to give."

-Boseman accepting the Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance by a Cast in a Motion Picture in 2019
